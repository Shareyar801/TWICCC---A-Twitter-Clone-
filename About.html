ABOUT US:

                    Welcome to our Twitter clone website! Our platform is built using Node.js, 
                    Express API, MongoDB, Tailwind CSS, and Bootstrap to deliver an exceptional 
                    user experience.
                    
                    At our core, we aim to provide a seamless social media experience, allowing 
                    users to connect, share their thoughts, and engage in meaningful conversations. 
                    With the power of Node.js and Express API, we have developed a robust and scalable
                    backend that handles user authentication, data storage, and real-time updates.
                    
                    Our website leverages MongoDB, a flexible and efficient NoSQL database, to store 
                    user profiles, tweets, and other essential data. This allows for quick retrieval 
                    and seamless integration with our backend services.
                    
                    For the front-end design, we have combined the power of Tailwind CSS and Bootstrap. 
                    These frameworks offer a wide range of customizable components, responsive layouts, 
                    and beautiful styling options to enhance the user interface and make
                    navigation intuitive and enjoyable.
